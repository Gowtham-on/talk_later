Privacy Policy – Talk Later

Last updated: 2024-07-01

1. Information We Collect

a. Device Permissions
To provide our core features, Talk Later requests access to:

  • Call Logs: To detect missed and rejected calls, show reminders, and help you call back.
  • Contacts: To display the name of callers in your reminders and notifications.
  • Notifications: To remind you to call back and add notes.
  • Other Permissions: (If applicable) For notifications, boot completion (to reschedule reminders), and app maintenance.

b. Personal Information
We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from your device on our servers. All call log and contact information stays locally on your device.

c. Usage Data
We do not use analytics or tracking tools that collect your app usage or personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To remind you to call back missed or rejected calls.
  • To show the caller’s name (from your contacts) in reminders and notifications.
  • To help you schedule follow-up reminders (with optional notes).
  • To keep your reminders up to date, and remove old reminders automatically.

We do not share or upload your information to any third parties or external servers.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All call, contact, and reminder data is stored securely on your device only.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not upload your data to any cloud service or server.
  • Automatic Deletion: Old reminder data is periodically deleted for your privacy.

4. Third-Party Services

Talk Later does not integrate with any third-party advertising, analytics, or social sharing services.

5. User Control

  • You can revoke permissions at any time via your device settings.
  • You can delete all reminder data from the app (via settings).
  • You can uninstall the app at any time; this deletes all data stored by the app.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the app and on our website.
